    Preface

    It’s time to realize that “I” exist in “We”. It’s time to use our faith, our hope and our courage for our dignified identity of mankind. Let’s decide what we will do and what we will never do. Let’s try to be better and revive that sharing happiness and living for each other is our identity. Let us not forget, there are no chosen ones, it’s just about the choices one makes. Each does exist in the uniqueness of diversity. It’s time for us to remind that patience for pain, tolerance for sorrow, humility for happiness, sentiment for sadness and humour for foolishness are our greatest virtues.

    We are together here because we have a dream. Dream of an empowered society and an amazing nature to live with… We believe that we need to make necessary adjustments for a much secure future. We believe that steps should be taken as our previous generations did for the betterment of society. We should accept the necessity and act upon it. Mother Nature is lonely. Her children are being extinct and undergoing sufferings because of mankind’s actions. Air and water-borne diseases are everywhere. Humanity is facing hunger and scarcity in this twenty-first century. We don’t have a proper crisis plan for all. Mankind is still dying in the lack of medical support. And again children, maternity cases, disabled and elderly people are waiting for extra addressing that we are busy with self-existence, power, ego and fame. And above all, we have a large number of less & semi-skilled people, under-educated society and very fewer intellect people which at a time-frame could cause a formation of a void, affecting the productivity and performance of mankind. How would the upcoming generation be groomed up in this context! It’s time to act to fulfil god’s will and not to fall into the trap of the word “freedom”.

    Doing everything for our best adaptation and improving our way of approach of co-existence entirely depends upon the awareness that we flourish because of Water, Air and Soil. So taking these essential elements in mind as we upgrade ourselves is the best way to heal and revive ourselves. We all know we should let the universe act as well while we act. Let’s focus on an individual’s necessity towards preserving nature and thinking about society.
