Frequently Asked Questions

  • How do you take care of children ?

    First of all we fight against children abuses such as Child labor and buying selling of children.Other factors include stopping child marraige and supporting children who cannot feed themselves properly or study.

  • Are you working on an orphanage?

    Yes, we are working on orphanage. The orphanage is to be taken on two floors with 4 rooms on each floor. A seperate canteen is to be placed for each meal time, and we are also working on opening a low-cost montessary school after the orphanage.

  • How do you promote social service?

    Society is a must for anone, whether people or animals. In society we learn to co-exist, so it is important for us to work combined and so that no one has to face a crisis alone. When anyone gets into troubled situation whether it is caused by social issues such as discrimination or natural crisis such as landslide/ flood, we consider to offer help as a fellow human being.

  • Are all of your products organic?

    100%. All of our process, not only products are organic. We have our own cow farm and mushroom field, which help us produce our own manure. The seeds we produce are sure to be better grown products than your average market seeds if taken care of properly.

  • Do you have any other works you need to mention?

    Reasearch is a primary section we are intending to watch over. There is also bio-technological experiments we are going to carry out once the reasearch fund budget is met.
